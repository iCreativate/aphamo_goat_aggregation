Quality goats, from farm to market—with training, oversight, and fair access for all.

Our Strategic Focus Areas

Innovative approaches to goat aggregation excellence

Quality & Training

We combine rigorous quality standards with training for beneficiaries and farmers. From animal health and veterinary oversight to best practice in husbandry and handling, we ensure goats meet market requirements and our partners are equipped to sustain high standards.

Market Access

We connect farmers directly to buyers—retail, wholesale, and agro-processors—reducing the role of middlemen and improving returns. Our aggregation model coordinates collection, slaughter, and processing so more of the value stays with producers.

Value for Beneficiaries

Through fair pricing, technical support, and training, we help emerging farmers and beneficiaries build sustainable goat enterprises. The hub provides a reliable outlet and the skills needed to supply quality animals and benefit from the full value chain.

Breeds We Work With

From premium meat breeds to hardy indigenous stock—we aggregate and support the major South African goat breeds.

Boer World-renowned meat breed. Excellent feed conversion and carcass yield. Kalahari Red Hardy, heat- and drought-resistant. Strong in harsh conditions. Savanna White, drought-resistant. Lean carcasses, good for intensive or extensive systems. Indigenous Veld Nguni, Cape Lob Ear, Speckled, Kunene. Hardy, disease-resistant, culturally valued.
