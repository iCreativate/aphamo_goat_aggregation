Training & Development

Structured programmes to build skills in goat production, dairy, and value addition—from farm to market.

Skills for the full value chain

Aphamo Boerdery provides structured, practical training so that emerging farmers, beneficiaries, and cooperatives can improve production, manage dairy and meat operations, and add value through processing. Our programmes combine on-site learning, classroom workshops, and ongoing mentorship to support sustainable goat enterprises.

Farmer Training Programmes

Aphamo Boerdery provides structured training in the following areas, designed to build capacity from basics through to value addition and market readiness.

1

Goat Production Training

Foundational skills for raising goats for meat and breeding—from housing and feeding to health and records.

  • Housing & Infrastructure
  • Feeding & Nutrition
  • Breeding & Selection
  • Health Management
  • Record Keeping
  • Financial Management
2

Dairy Goat Training

Best practice in milking, hygiene, storage, and the basics of dairy processing for goat milk enterprises.

  • Milking procedures
  • Hygiene & sanitation
  • Milk storage
  • Processing basics
3

Value Addition Training

From cheese and meat processing to packaging, labelling, and food safety—so you can capture more value.

  • Cheese making
  • Meat processing
  • Packaging & labeling
  • Food safety compliance
