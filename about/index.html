About Aphamo

Aphamo Aggregation Centre is a Goat Excellence Centre dedicated to breeding, production, training, and aggregation of high-quality goats and goat products. We connect farmers with markets, support skills development, and strengthen the goat value chain across South Africa through Koedoeberg Farm and Aphamo Boerdery.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

Aphamo is committed to empowering farmers and communities across South Africa through quality breeding, training, and aggregation. We specialise in goat excellence—breeding, production, and market access—to transform livelihoods and build a sustainable goat value chain.

Our mission is to provide a trusted platform for listings, breed knowledge, and skills development (free and premium training) so that emerging farmers, beneficiaries, cooperatives, and buyers can access genetics, learn best practices, and connect to markets.

Our Vision

Aphamo envisions becoming the leading and most trusted Goat Aggregation and Excellence Centre in South Africa, recognised for breeding quality, training excellence, and market linkages.

We strive to be at the forefront of goat sector development, empowering farmers and cooperatives through our platform and Koedoeberg Farm, and building lasting partnerships that drive sustainable growth and transformation across the industry.
