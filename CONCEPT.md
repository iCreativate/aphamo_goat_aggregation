# Aphamo Goat Aggregation – Website Concept & Specification

**South Africa's Premier Goat Aggregation & Learning Hub**

---

## 1. Executive Summary

Aphamo Goat Aggregation positions itself as the **#1 integrated platform** for South African goat farmers, breeders, and buyers—combining a trusted marketplace (better than Digikraal), deep breed education (beyond boergoats.co.za and indigenousveldgoats.co.za), and structured training (superior to Regvic Stud, GASA, InvestaGoat) in one mobile-first, POPIA-compliant experience.

**Target audience:** Farmers and breeders from Gauteng, Free State, Limpopo, KZN, Eastern Cape, Northern Cape, and beyond—from beginners to commercial producers.  
**Breeds in focus:** Boer, Kalahari Red, Savanna, Indigenous Veld (Nguni/Mbuzi, Cape Lob Ear, Northern Cape Speckled, Kunene).

## 4. Color Palette, Fonts & Icon Style

### 4.1 Color Palette

| Role | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|--------|
| **Primary – Savanna orange** | `#C45C26` | Main CTAs, key headings, links |
| **Primary dark** | `#9B3D1A` | Hover states, emphasis |
| **Secondary – Veld green** | `#4A7C59` | Secondary buttons, success, nature |
| **Secondary dark** | `#2D5A3A` | Footer, dark green accents |
| **Terracotta** | `#B85C38` | Backgrounds, cards, warmth |
| **Warm cream** | `#F5EDE4` | Page backgrounds, sections |
| **Earthy brown** | `#5C4033` | Body text, icons |
| **Gold / energy** | `#D4A84B` | Badges, highlights, "Premium" |
| **Neutral dark** | `#2C2C2C` | Headings, footer text |
| **White** | `#FFFFFF` | Cards, nav, contrast |
| **Border / subtle** | `#E0D5CA` | Dividers, input borders |

**Accessibility:** Ensure contrast ratios ≥ 4.5:1 for body text, ≥ 3:1 for large text and UI (WCAG 2.1).

### 4.2 Typography

- **Headings:** Sans-serif – **Poppins** (Bold 700, SemiBold 600) for impact and clarity.
- **Body:** **Inter** (Regular 400, Medium 500) for readability.
- **Fallbacks:** `system-ui, -apple-system, sans-serif`.
- **Scale:** e.g. H1 2.25–2.5rem, H2 1.75–2rem, H3 1.25–1.5rem, body 1rem, small 0.875rem.
- **Line height:** 1.5–1.6 body; 1.2–1.3 headings.

### 4.3 Icon Style

- **Style:** Rounded, friendly, 24px default. Outline (stroke) preferred; solid for filled states (e.g. favorites, active nav).
- **Library suggestion:** Lucide, Heroicons, or Phosphor – consistent weight (1.5–2px stroke).
- **Use:** Navigation, filters, cards (location, price, WhatsApp), buttons, stats.

## 6. Key Unique Selling Points (Why #1 in SA)

1. **All-in-one platform** – Marketplace + education + training + community in one place (vs separate Digikraal, Regvic, GASA, breed sites).
2. **Trust & verification** – Verified breeder badges, clear seller profiles, optional health certs in filters; POPIA-compliant and transparent data use.
3. **SA-first content** – Breeds, provinces, ZAR, local vets/experts, WhatsApp, in-person workshops in Gauteng/Pretoria and beyond.
4. **Free + premium path** – Free listings and free starter courses lower the barrier; premium listings and courses fund quality and growth.
5. **Learning that ties to selling** – Certificates and herd tracker in The Hub; "See listings for this breed" on breed pages; courses mention marketplace.
6. **Mobile-first & fast** – Built for phones first; fast SA hosting; WhatsApp for inquiries.
7. **SEO & discoverability** – Targeted titles (e.g. "Goats for Sale Gauteng", "Free Goat Farming Course South Africa") and structured content for search and ads.
8. **Community & support** – Forums, live Q&A, mentor directory, success stories—reduces isolation and builds loyalty.

## 7. Monetization (Hints)

- **Listings:** Free basic listings; premium/featured listings (e.g. longer visibility, more photos, map highlight) for a fee.
- **Training:** One-time purchase (R500–R1500) or subscription for premium course bundles.
- **Partnerships:** Breed societies, feed companies, vet practices, auctions—sponsored content or featured placement.
- **Optional:** Commission on completed sales (clearly disclosed) or lead fees.

---

## 8. Technical & Compliance

- **POPIA:** Privacy policy, consent for marketing and WhatsApp, data retention, right to deletion.
- **SSL:** HTTPS everywhere.
- **Hosting:** Fast, SA or nearby (e.g. AWS af-south-1, or local provider) for low latency.
- **Performance:** Lazy-loaded images, minimal JS above the fold, critical CSS inlined if needed; target LCP < 2.5s on 3G.
