Become a Beneficiary

Aphamo Boerdery empowers subsistence and smallholder farmers through a structured beneficiary model.

Beneficiary Requirements

To join the programme, applicants are expected to meet the following:

  • Commitment to training
  • Repurposing of unproductive land
  • Basic infrastructure readiness
  • Willingness to follow standard procedures
  • Participation in aggregation programme

Beneficiary Process

From application through to market integration.

  • 1
    Application Personal details, farming experience, land availability
  • 2
    Assessment Site visit, infrastructure evaluation, commitment verification
  • 3
    Training Production, compliance, and record keeping training
  • 4
    Integration Supply agreement, quality standards, market integration

Beneficiary Code of Conduct

All beneficiaries must adhere to the following standards:

  • Maintain animal welfare standards
  • Follow veterinary protocols
  • Keep accurate production records
  • Sell through approved aggregation channels
  • Keep financial records
