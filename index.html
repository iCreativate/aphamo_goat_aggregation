Breeds We Support

From commercial Boer and Kalahari Red to hardy Indigenous Veld—learn traits, management, and find listings.

Boer

World-renowned meat breed. Excellent feed conversion and carcass yield. Adaptable across SA.

Kalahari Red

Hardy, heat- and drought-resistant. Red coat, strong in harsh conditions.

Savanna

White, drought-resistant. Lean carcasses, good for intensive or extensive systems.

Indigenous Veld

Nguni, Cape Lob Ear, Speckled, Kunene. Hardy, disease-resistant, culturally valued.
